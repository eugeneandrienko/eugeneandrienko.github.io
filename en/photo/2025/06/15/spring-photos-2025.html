Experiments with CGA/EGA and dithering

Also, I performed an experiments with a reduced color palettes and dithering after reading some articles on the "Hundredrabbits" website.

All these transformation were made inside Gimp with the help of some palettes from these sources:

  • GIMP color palettes from retro systems, anime, etc
  • A collection of color palettes for GIMP and Inkscape.

These palettes were placed to the ~/.config/GIMP/2.10/palettes/. Each palette can be applied via the "Image⇒Mode⇒Indexed" dialog box (see the "Use custom palette" radio button).

The dithering can be applied also via the same dialog.
